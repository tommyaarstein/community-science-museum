Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

Our opening hours varies per day. Please visit tcsm.no/visit for more information.

What is the entry fee?

Adults: Kr 200,-

Children: Kr 100,-

Children 0-5: Free

Where are you located?

We are located at Kvaløyvegen 11, 9019 Tromsø. You can also find a map at tcsm.no/visit.

Can we bring our children free of charge?

Children aged 0 to 5 have free entry. Children above the age of 5 is Kr 100,-.

Do you sell food and drinks?

Yes! Our Cafè is located on the 1st floor next to the reception. You can buy coffee, soft drinks, snacks and pre-made food.

Send us a message:

If you have any other questions, feel free to submit your contact information and question below.
We will answer as soon as possible. You can expect an answer within 1 business day.
